Meet Our Faculty

Mahesh Jammula

Head of Department

Mr. Mahesh Jammula is an alumnus of NIFT, Hyderabad. He and his creative zeal has taken him to all corners of Fashion Industry. Gaining experience from well-known Designers, Film Industry, Ministry of Textiles and Television /Media made him a versatile designer. He started a studio "NUTAN DESIGNS" in 2007 and his own label called "MAHESH JAMMULA" in 2010. With his unique and assuring style of teaching, he made a mark as a "Fashion Guru" in teaching field. He specializes in Drapes, Advanced Garment Construction and Fashion Styling. He mentored many fashion shows and events yielding excellent results from students.

Mr. Syed Anwaruddin Mr. Syed Anwaruddin is an expert faculty in textile & fashion technology. On other hand he deals in Marketing courses as well. Having multiple Degrees and masters in his hand, he made a bench mark to students to join in dual courses. 10 years in teaching sector & 15 years in apparel industry experience make him infinity knowledge on his finger tips. He is also certified as Assessor from Ministry of textiles to guide women's empowerment orientated business.
