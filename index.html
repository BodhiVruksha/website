Courses Offered

Fashion Designing

The program is designed in a way that allows the students to identify areas of interest within the fashion segments of textiles and transition into desired path. The program aims to equip the student with an in-depth understanding of the role of the fashion designer..

More Info.
Interior Designing

The program aims to develop the ability to conceptualize, plan, design, detailed functional and aesthetical interior spaces ranging from small residential spaces to large public & commercial spaces. It trains you to creatively interpret cultural contexts..

More Info.
Product Designing

The program is an interdisciplinary platform for young creatives to learn how to design product & accessories and transition into desired path. The program aims to equip the student with an in-depth understanding of the role of the product designer..

More Info.
Finishing School

A thoughtfully tailored course which focuses on teaching social graces and upper-class cultural rites as a preparation for entry into the modern society, with expert advice on Appearance, Behaviour, Communication Skills and Self Management..
