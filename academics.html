Fashion Designing

We provide professional training in the field of Fashion Technology by formulating well-crafted curricula that aims to develop professional expertise and aesthetic-aptitude required to become a Fashion Designer par excellence in the Design & Fashion Industry. Our curriculum enables the students to acquire greater appreciation for design, develop a deeper sensibility towards fashion aesthetics and nurtures their individual creative potentials.

Syllabus

  • Basic Designing
  • Fashion Styling
  • Fashion Illustration
  • Pattern Making
  • Garment Construction
  • Computer Application of Fashion
  • Textiles
  • History of Fashion
  • Marketing & Merchandizing etc..

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Designers
  • Textile Designers
  • Costume Designers
  • Stylists
  • Illustrators

Interior Designing

The Interior Design pathway focuses more on aesthetics and styles of interiors, with an approach toward themes, decor and styling. It is a very expressive art and requires designers to be constantly on their feet with innovative ideas and creative inputs. The Interior Design courses in Bodhi Vruksha is focused on creative planning and skilled design space ideas.

Syllabus

  • Basic Designing
  • Design Drawings & Graphics
  • History of Interior Design
  • Technical Services
  • Materials
  • Construction
  • Interior Design Studio etc..

Career Opportunities

  • Interior Designers
  • Interior Decorators
  • Set Designers
  • Furniture Designers

Product Designing

This program trains an individual to become a jewelry or accessory designer by enabling them to acquire the skills and techniques. Product Designing course at Bodhi Vruksha is the epitome of excellence in delivery, facilitating the learning of students by generously exposing them to the traditional crafts of India alongside technology, and inspiring their engagement with the creation of captivating objects of desire.

Syllabus

  • Introduction to the course
  • Introducion to innovation process
  • Creative idea generation
  • Concept selection
  • Manufacturing methods
  • Prototyping exercise

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Jewelry Designers
  • Accessory Designers

Finishing School

Our college is first of its kind in the city to have specialized course as Finishing School. Designed on the lines of the twelve-week course. This module is designed to fit into your busy lives. This three month program is an intensive course where students will be rigorously trained for 40-45 hours weekly, along with weekly assignments and practical examinations. Personality enhancement, achieving the optimal work life balance, fine dining, personality grooming and branding, effective communication and cross culture training are some of the topics that will be covered. This unique curriculum aims at endowing individuals with self-reliance, confidence and empowerment in their personal and professional lives, while helping them to identify their potential and creating a successful path for their future.
